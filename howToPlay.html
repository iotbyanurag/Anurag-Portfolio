RGB COLOR GAME TUTORIAL

This page is a tutorial for playing the game, it tells you about the concept of color-mixing in the digital-domain, where every pixel is made up of a RGB LED. Digital colors are constructed by mixing three primary colors.

  • Red + Green = Yellow
  • Red + Blue = Purple
  • Green + Blue = Cyan (blue-green)
  • Red + Green + Blue = White
  • no colors = Black
